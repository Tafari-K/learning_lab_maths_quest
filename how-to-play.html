How to Play

Getting Started

Welcome to the Learning Lab Math Quest! Follow these steps to start your adventure:

  1. Select a math challenge, to begin the game.
  2. Answer the math question
  3. When you have finished, you will see your score.

Playing the Game

Once the game starts, you'll be presented with random math questions. Here's how to play:

  • Read the question carefully.
  • Type your answer in the provided input box.
  • Click "Submit" to see if you're correct!
  • If your answer is correct you will earn points
  • If your answer is incorrect, you will be asked to try again.
  • At the end you will be asked for your name and this will used to send your scores to your teacher/parent.
